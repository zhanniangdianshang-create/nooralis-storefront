Technical specifications

Lightweight, portable, and tuned for trained operators.

Specifications are based on the LED bionic butterfly flying device configuration. Actual flight time depends on LED use, air movement, battery condition, and operator handling.

Product type Remote controlled bionic flapping-wing butterfly
Wingspan 50 x 70 cm
Weight Approx. 60 g
Battery 2S 180 mAh rechargeable lithium battery
Charging USB charging, 5 V input only, approx. 30-40 minutes
Flight time Approx. 5 minutes with LED lights, approx. 7 minutes without LED lights
Control range Up to 200 m, recommended within visual range
Flight environment Indoor or calm outdoor flight. Avoid wind, rain, and high humidity.
Recommended altitude Within 30 m for event operation
Options LED version, standard wing version, custom color and pattern requests

Operator notes

  • Pairing is configured before shipment.
  • Reduce throttle before landing or impact.
  • Do not keep wings flapping after landing.
  • Use trained operators for paid events.

FAQ

Questions buyers ask before ordering.

Is this suitable for beginners?

It can be learned with practice, but paid events should use a trained operator and rehearsal time.

Can it fly outdoors?

Yes, only in calm conditions. Wind, rain, and high humidity can reduce control and damage the airframe.

Can the wings be customized?

Yes. Standard colors are available, and custom color or pattern requests can be quoted.

How long is each flight?

Typical flight time is about 5 minutes with LED lights on, or about 7 minutes without LED lights.
