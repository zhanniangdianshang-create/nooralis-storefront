Nooralis policies

Shipping, refunds, privacy, and purchase terms.

These policies apply to online orders for Nooralis LED bionic butterfly drone products. For custom event, bulk, or urgent delivery orders, contact us before payment so we can confirm availability and timing.

Shipping Policy

Orders are prepared after payment confirmation and paid-order detail review. Processing time depends on inventory, color selection, customization, and destination. International delivery times vary by country, customs handling, and carrier availability.

Buyers are responsible for providing a complete and accurate shipping address during PayPal checkout. Import taxes, duties, customs fees, or local clearance costs may be charged by the destination country and are the buyer's responsibility unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Single-kit orders are reviewed after the store receives the paid-order form. Exact dispatch timing is confirmed by destination, stock status, and courier route.

Refund And Return Policy

If an order cannot be fulfilled, we will contact the buyer and issue a refund through the original PayPal payment method. For change-of-mind returns, contact us within 7 days of delivery before sending anything back.

Returned products must be unused, complete, and in original packaging. Custom colors, custom patterns, used products, crash-damaged products, and event-used products may not be eligible for return unless required by applicable law or confirmed defective on arrival.

For damaged-on-arrival, missing parts, or wrong-configuration cases, use the Nooralis returns page so the request can be matched with the order record and reviewed by the service desk.

Privacy Policy

We collect the information needed to process orders, respond to inquiries, arrange shipping, and provide customer support. This may include name, email address, shipping details, order selections, and messages submitted through email or checkout.

Payments are processed by PayPal. We do not store full card details on this website. We may use order and inquiry information to provide service, prevent fraud, comply with legal obligations, and improve the storefront.

Terms Of Purchase

Nooralis butterfly drones are performance props that require careful handling, safe operating conditions, and rehearsal before paid events. Buyers are responsible for local compliance, venue permission, safe use, and trained operation.

Product specifications, prices, and availability may change. We reserve the right to refuse or refund orders that cannot be safely or reasonably fulfilled. Contact: zhannaingdianshang@gmail.com.
